INTERNATIONARY

Find Support, Information, Advice, and Solutions - All in One Place

settle fast

We can help your process of settling down become easier and quicker by providing you with affordable living options. From finding an on-campus dorm to looking for an off-campus apartment we are here to provide you with the best resources so that you can evaluate your options wisely.

Travel Safe

We understand that moving to a new place is difficult. To make travelling easier for you we provide you with both public and private transportation options in Seattle. Whether you choose to ride the bus or rent a car, we want to make sure you are aware of the best options to ensure a smooth and safe travel.

Manage Finances

To make sure you manage your finances wisely, we help you understand what credit score is and why it matters. We also provide information on how to apply for student loans as an international student and help you get started on finding a bank that fits your needs.

Get the right healthcare

Health is a priority and we want to make sure every student is aware of the breadth of healthcare resources available to them as a student at the University. We help you find both physical and mental health resources. To ensure you make the best use of your insurance, we offer information on the benefits and services your health insurance covers.

About Us

We are a group of international students from the Information school at the University of Washington, passionate about making a difference in the experience of the international community at the UW. We understand that moving to a totally different country, education system and culture can be very confusing and daunting. There is a lot of information that international students need to be able to adjust to this new place. Researching and collecting information is a cumbersome process and students might miss some useful information or get the wrong information which might hurt them in the long run.

To solve this problem, we made Internationary, a one stop website which students can use to easily get information about important things and adapt well to this new place and system and in turn succeed in their lives! We provide information about healthcare, finances, housing and transportation which are the 4 essential pillars of adapting in any new place. We provide information from trusted sources which will help students make important decisions on their own, using the information that we provide.

OUR MISSION

To help all international students at the University of Washington feel confident to live and study here in Seattle.
We aim to do this through our portal by giving out knowledge about everything those students need to know about successfully navigating life here in a foriegn country, culture and education system.
